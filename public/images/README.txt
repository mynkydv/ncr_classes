Drop your photos here. The site picks them up automatically —
no code change needed.

  hero.jpg     Portrait, 4:5 (e.g. 1200 x 1500).
               A real tutor mid-lesson with a student at a dining table.
               Warm daylight, faces visible, not posed.

  teacher.jpg  Landscape, 4:3 (e.g. 1600 x 1200).
               One of your actual tutors, smiling, at a desk or whiteboard.

Until a file exists, the site shows a hatched placeholder with these
instructions written on it, so nothing looks broken.

Two rules:
  1. Phone photos in good daylight are fine. Stock photos of American
     teenagers will look fake to an NCR parent and will hurt you.
  2. Get WRITTEN consent from a parent before photographing any child,
     and prefer shots where the child is from behind or out of focus.

Keep files under ~400 KB. Squoosh.app compresses well.
